Understanding Credit Reports

A credit report is a detailed record of your credit history maintained by credit bureaus. It includes information such as your open and closed credit accounts, payment history, outstanding debts, credit inquiries, and any negative records like defaults or bankruptcies. Lenders use this report to assess your financial behavior before approving loans or credit cards. Regularly reviewing your credit report helps identify errors, detect fraud, and stay informed about your financial health. It's a key tool for managing and improving your credit score.
